1. Product Overview
The Lumino Hub is a centralized smart home controller. It connects via Wi-Fi (2.4GHz and 5GHz) and supports Zigbee and Z-Wave protocols. The device features a physical "Privacy Toggle" on the back that manually disconnects the microphone.

2. Troubleshooting Connection Issues
Red Blinking Light: This indicates a Wi-Fi authentication error. Ensure the password is correct and the router is within 10 meters.
Solid Orange Light: The Hub is performing a firmware update. Do not unplug the device.
Factory Reset: To reset, hold the "Sync" button for 15 seconds while plugging the power cable in. This will erase all paired devices.

4. Privacy and Security
Data Storage: User voice recordings are encrypted locally and deleted after 24 hours.
Remote Access: To enable remote control, users must activate "Cloud Bridge" in the Lumino App settings under the Security tab.
