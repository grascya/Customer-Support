11. Lumino Sentinel Plus
While basic features are free, "Sentinel Plus" ($4.99/mo) provides:
Extended Storage: 30 days of encrypted cloud video storage for Lumino Cameras.
AI Detection: Distinguishes between people, pets, and packages.
Cellular Backup: Keeps the hub online during Wi-Fi outages using the built-in LTE chip (North America only).

12. Billing and Account Management
Subscriptions are managed via the Lumino Web Portal. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are provided for mid-month cancellations.
